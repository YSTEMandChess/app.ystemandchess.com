Y STEM and Chess Inc. (YSC) is focused on resolving two current social problems, by providing a unique 21st Century methodology to learning called “Mastery.” The first focus is on our society’s unfulfilled need for students with a firm learning foundation to pursue a life in the world of STEM. The second and the primary focus of YSC is fulfilling those needs, by working with the children of our socially and economically underserved population, to introduce and engage them in the world of STEM.

Opening minds and hearts

Our current education system was created to satisfy the needs of an Industrial Age not those of the 21st Century. Our society from the latter part of the 1800s to mid-1960s focused on educating everyone to meet the literacy requirements in reading, writing, and math. The problem we face as a society is that our legacy education systems no longer fit the individual learning needs and haven’t since the 1960s that don’t include computers. Y STEM and Chess Inc. is a 501C3 organization formed to provide a solution to this problem with a focus on those disenfranchised children in our society that have been written off because of social and economic factors. Y STEM and Chess Inc was started by someone who was classified as not “educatable” by the system, but thanks to the determination of he and his family, achieved academic success with a BA in Philosophy and MS degree in Cybersecurity. He was not considered “educatable” above a 3rd or 4th-grade level by the school system. When forming the organization, the founder also recruited people with experience to solve the problem to help develop the YSC learning program. The first is a public school math teacher with over 30 years of experience in low income areas.
